Our Mission

The Challenge

There is a pressing need for basic infrastructure and community projects in developing countries. NGOs often have the will and a presence on the ground but lack the funding. Technology can help connect willing donors to NGOs that are ready to utilize the funding for the biggest impact .

Our Solution

Change 4 Change is a crowdfunding website that leverages the power of cloud computing to allow NGOs to propose funding campaigns and provide donors the tools they need to make informed decisions on where to donate to make the maximum impact in helping the world's poor.
